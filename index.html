About LHEssay

Lexington High School is stressful enough without worrying about poor writing grades. Meet LHEssay, a student-run organization that remedies unsatisfactory essay performance from within. We harness the skill of our school’s strongest writers to assist any students in seek of literary improvement. Request an edit today, and take your writing to the next level!

Who are we?

LHEssay is run by a small band of committed Lexington High School upperclassmen. Being a school-specific organization, every component of LHEssay is managed by active LHS students—from the talented programmers in charge of our website to the skilled writers who edit submissions. We are not private tutors or independent professionals; we are fellow LHS students, using our personal strengths to help our peers.
