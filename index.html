About LHEssay

Are you losing sleep over your writing grades? Meet LHEssay, a student-run organization that remedies unsatisfactory essay performance from within. We harness the skill of our school’s strongest writers to assist any students seeking literary improvement. Request an edit today, and take your writing to the next level!

Who are we?

Every component of LHEssay is managed by a select group of current LHS upperclassmen. From the talented programmers running our website to the skilled writers editing submissions, LHEssay’s staff provides quality assistance in a school-specific context.

How does it work?

Step 1 Step 2 Step 3 Step 4 Step 5
Enter your paper’s word count to receive an estimated return date and edit cost.
Attach your essay by providing the link to an online document. Ensure that editing permissions are enabled for link sharing. In the “comments” box, mention any word or page limit for the assignment. Click “submit” to send your request to our LHEssay editors for approval.
Our editors process your request, taking an official word count and checking your submission to ensure that it is a legitimate draft. We will edit submissions of most qualities, but we will not write an entire assignment for you. If your essay is approved, you will receive an email with a confirmed return date and price.
Upon receiving your order confirmation, complete the indicated online payment. In order to protect our editors’ time, we will not edit an essay until we have received payment. Our prices are always transparent, with a flat rate of 0.045 cents per word—for reference, a 650-word college essay would cost just under $30.
Relax. Once your payment is complete, your order becomes official. Sit back and allow our editors to do what they do best; when your paper is returned, it will be ready to turn in.

Why choose us?

At LHEssay, we value ease of access. With an entirely online submission and payment process, LHEssay ensures that you get the help you need whenever and wherever you may need it.
