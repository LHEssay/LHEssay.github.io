About LHEssay

Are you losing sleep over your writing grades? Meet LHEssay, a student-run organization that remedies unsatisfactory essay performance from within Lexington High School itself. We harness the skill of our school’s strongest writers to assist any students seeking literary improvement. Request an edit today and take your writing to the next level!

Who are we?

Every component of LHEssay is managed by a select group of current Lexington High School upperclassmen. Our years at LHS have given us a good picture of the school’s many intricacies, allowing us to help our peers with confident expertise. We also appreciate the value of bringing different personal skills to the table; from the talented programmers running our website to the skilled writers editing submissions, LHEssay’s staff provides quality assistance in a school-specific context.

How does it work?

Step 1 Step 2 Step 3 Step 4 Step 5
Word Count: Enter your paper’s word count to receive an estimated return date and edit cost.
Submission: Attach your essay and ensure that editing permissions are enabled for link sharing. In the “comments” box, name any word limit for the assignment. Click “submit” to send your request to our LHEssay editors for approval.
Editor Approval: Our editors process your request, taking an official word count and checking your submission to ensure that it is a legitimate draft. If your essay is approved, you will receive an email with a confirmed return date and price.
Verification & Payment: Upon receiving your order confirmation, complete the indicated online payment. We will not edit an essay until we have received payment. Our prices are always transparent, with a flat rate of 4 cents per word—a 650-word college essay would cost just $26.
Relax: Once your payment is complete, your order becomes official. Sit back and allow our editors to do what they do best; when your paper is returned, it will be ready to turn in.

Why choose us?

At LHEssay, we value ease of access. With an entirely online submission and payment process, LHEssay ensures that you get the help you need whenever and wherever you may need it. Our handpicked editors—each with a lifetime of experience in the Lexington school system—are all exceptionally qualified, with honors English experience and a 99+ verbal SAT percentile. We do not pretend to be professionals, but we can say with certainty that your work will be returned with a quality comparable to that of a professional service.

What can you submit?
  • You can submit almost any type of writing: creative stories, analytical paragraphs, and college essays are just a few examples of submissions that we will gladly edit.
  • You cannot submit incomplete assignments and expect us to finish them. For example, if we receive an essay without a conclusion, we will edit what is given but we will not conclude the essay for you. With our price based on word count, we must receive a conclusion if we are to edit it.
  • Important note: we can only make a piece of writing as good as its draft. We don’t write essays for you; we make essays sound good. With a school assignment, it is your responsibility to select an appropriate topic and utilize strong evidence.
